Hospital Readmissions Analysis

This project explores customer purchasing behaviour within an e-commerce platform to uncover revenue drivers and retention patterns. Using python, data was cleaned, merged, and analyzed to generate key business KPIs such as revenue trends. Customers were segmented using RFM analysis to identify high-value and at-risk groups.
